How to Order

  1. Click WhatsApp and send your logo (black & white preferred).
  2. Tell us size, surface (paper/plastic), and quantity.
  3. We confirm price & prep a mockup for approval.
  4. Payment via Shopee/transfer. We produce & ship.

FAQ

What file format do you need?

Vector (SVG/AI/PDF) is best. High-res black & white PNG also works.

Can it stamp on plastic?

Yes, with dry-fast ink and the right stamp pad. Let us know your use case.

How long to ship?

Usually 3–5 working days after artwork approval.
